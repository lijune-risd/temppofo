CVS Bloodflow

Branding Project

CVS Bloodflow is a fictional medicine for patients with low blood pressure. As an extension of CVS Health, this fictional medicine becomes a branding project that exoeriments with the element of blood vessels. By taking imagintive liberties, I created the blood ellipses to ballooned ellipses. The text in the back are pushed to back to create perspective. The text include words such as "Blast", "Blood", and "Beyond" in order to create a sense of power in this blood pressure medicine. These elements compound to create a three-dimensional design that would be the base of this CVS fictional identity.
