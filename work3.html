Youtube Redesign

UI UX Project

Youtube UIUX Redesign aims to reorganize the UI based on the feedback from its current users. By understanding the pros and cons of the design, we aim to create a more succesful design for the users than previously before. We hope that the app includes features that is inclusive, includes a focus on featured playlist, and extends the brand identity of Youtube.

Problem

Youtube Music -- a music streaming platform -- lacks differentiation and vision comparitively from the other brands.

Goals

1. Redesign for better user usage space for video and music
2. Include Humanistic Design Approach
3. Redesign for individual-centered design experience

Competitve Analysis

Apple Music Spotify Youtube Music
UI UX Design Clean UIUX Design with heavy gradient use. Not a lot of buttons and kept the design to the minimum. Unlike Apple Music, utilizes dark mode design. Follows the trend of battery saving dark mode designs. Follows the brand design of green occaisionally. Youtube Music utilizes the dark mode design like Spotify.
Human Approach No function to increase lyric size. However, lyrics are big from the start. Has a voice feature that is able to track the words of the user. Thinks of a universal audience of minorities.However, font is small at times. Has a voice feature to catch directions from voice. Also utilizes stark colors to differentiate different section. However, comparitively, lacks the larger font-size that can make the lyrics accessible to people.
Music Video No Real option to see it through Though there are music video loops, spotify does not prioritize in creazting a music video experience. Has a strong music video section, but the section is within the home section and makes it hard to differentiate.

Ideation

Information Architecture

User Testing

After the first redesign, we took the designs back to the users. There were three major feedbacks we received:


1. Have a strong video and music button system.
2. Have more playlist features
3. Have simplified or engaging proflile section

Next Step

Following the problems, we created design solutions for the new UI UX.


Solution
Music Video Button Create a visible switch button with text inside
Profile Add different visual highlights and hiearchy to the design.
Playlist Add more sections that include different playlist (including a video highlight ) for favorite music videos and music.

Universal Design

We also thought about universal design and how we could accomodate larger font to the lyric page.

Reflection

Project allowed me to inspect the different aspects of creating a music streaming app services. Most importantly, we explored ways to reorganize the UI UX system to fit Youtube's identity as a video platform (while keeping its existing music streaming functionlity at its core).
